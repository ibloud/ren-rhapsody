The Map Blueprint

Players spawn in a monochrome world. Collecting "Beat Fragments" unlocks Ren's music, dynamically changing the environment, adding color, and triggering platforming challenges synced to the beat.

Gritty, urban, dimly lit. Brighton origins.

Music: "Old Boy", "Patricia's Manifesto"

Collab: Ruby Duff NPC grants the "Microphone Item" to shatter glass barriers.

Zone 2: Mind’s Theater

Surreal, psychological, theatrical.

Music: "Hi Ren", "Sick Boi"

Collab: Find floating masks representing vocal stems to solve puzzles and escape closing walls.

Zone 3: Animal Flow

Neon, jungle, fast-paced.

Music: "Animal Flow", "Illest of Our Time"

Zone 4: The Money Game

Lavish, chaotic casino.

Music: "Money Game" (Pts 1-3)

Zone 5: Symphony

Floating amphitheater in space.

Music: "The Hunger", "Road Runner", "Blue"

Collab: Every saved NPC appears on stage. Conduct the orchestra to ascend a staircase of musical notes.

Interactive Mechanics

Audio Reactive World

Using UEFN’s material editor, neon lights and glowing plants pulse, change color, and scale based on the frequencies of Ren’s tracks.

The "Ren Guitar"

A reskinned Guitar emote prop. Strumming creates a shockwave that destroys "writer's block" brick walls.

Deep Cut Easter Eggs

Walk backward for 10 seconds to find a room playing "Violet's Tale." Find a doctor's note for a "Heart of Gold" XP boost.

How to Make It Playable

  1. Finalize Core Settings: Open My Island > Game Settings. Set to "Create" mode, 1-4 players, and set respawns to "Ground Spawners."
  2. Upload Custom Audio: Import Ren's tracks (.wav/.ogg) into UEFN. Check "I do not own the rights to this audio" if publishing as a fan project to avoid monetization/copyright strikes, or submit a Rights Management Form if working with Ren's team.
  3. Configure Island Portal: Use the Island Starter Portal device and assign it to your Zone 1 starting spawn point.
  4. Publish in UEFN: Click "Publish Current Island" in the top toolbar. Wait for UEFN to compile the project to Epic's servers.
  5. Make Public: Go to creator.fortnite.com > Projects > Islands. Toggle visibility from "Private" to "Public" to generate your 9-digit Island Code.
  6. Optimize for Discovery: Add tags (Adventure, Music, Parkour). Upload a high-quality neon thumbnail.
  7. Share with the World: Post the Island Code and gameplay clips on the Ren Discord, r/FortniteCreative, and TikTok.
